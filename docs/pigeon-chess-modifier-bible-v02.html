MOD-A001 ≈ The Last of Us / Cordyceps meme
The Cordyceptic Spread
Tier S
▪ Board Mutation · Persistent · Mid-Match Trigger
TRIGGER: Every 5 turns
One random empty tile becomes Infected. Pieces standing on Infected tiles lose 1 square of movement per turn they remain stationary. A Bishop or Queen passing through clears the infection. Infected tiles spread: 25% chance to infect an adjacent tile each turn. The mushroom is patient. The mushroom always wins eventually.
"There is something growing on the board. Viktor has chosen not to investigate what it is."
Point Cost4 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Dark Board — hidden infected tiles are catastrophic
  • Conflict: The Floor Is Lava — infection cannot spread onto Lava tiles
MOD-A002 ≈ "The Floor Is Lava" meme / viral challenge
The Floor Is Lava
Tier A
▪ Board Mutation · Persistent · Rotating
TRIGGER: Match start + every 10 turns
Four tiles are Lava. Any piece landing on Lava is immediately captured — including your own. Every 10 turns, each Lava tile rotates clockwise one square. The next position is shown as an amber outline one turn in advance. Everyone knows the floor is lava. Nobody is handling it well.
"The floor is lava. Viktor's knight is on the floor. Viktor is processing this."
Point Cost3 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Trapdoor Tiles — a piece falling through a trapdoor onto Lava is captured
  • Conflict: The Cordyceptic Spread — Lava tiles cannot become Infected
MOD-A003 ≈ Among Us / Fall Guys trap mechanics
Vent Tiles
Tier B
▪ Board Mutation · Hidden · One-Time
TRIGGER: On piece landing
Three tiles are secretly Vents at match start. The first piece to land on a Vent gets ejected — teleporting to the diametrically opposite square. If that square is occupied, the arriving piece captures whatever is there regardless of colour. Once used, Vents become visible empty shafts (impassable). Nobody saw what happened. Nobody is talking about it.
"Viktor's bishop vanished and reappeared behind enemy lines. Viktor did not do that on purpose. Viktor is going to pretend he did."
Point Cost2 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Ghost Pieces — decoy pieces can trigger Vents and vanish convincingly
  • Synergy: Dark Board — neither player can track Vent positions in fog
MOD-A004 ≈ Game of Thrones / Winter Is Coming
Winter Is Coming (To Your Pieces)
Tier B
▪ Board Mutation · Persistent · Zone
TRIGGER: Match start (permanent)
Both home rows (Ranks 1–3 and 6–8) are Frozen. Any piece entering a Frozen zone must thaw for 1 full turn before moving again. Pawns freeze before promoting. Kings cannot castle if either castle-path tile is Frozen. The White Walkers are not here. That would be a different game. The cold, however, is very real.
"Viktor's entire back rank is frozen. A raven arrived to warn him. Viktor does not know how to talk to ravens."
Point Cost2 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: The Heavy Is The Crown — King thaws slower (2 turns), castling nearly impossible
  • Conflict: The Small Council (Peasant Revolt) — 12 pawns all thawing on home rows risks gridlock
MOD-A005 ≈ Minecraft world border / Fortnite storm
The Storm Closes In
Tier A
▪ Board Mutation · Shrinking · Progressive
TRIGGER: Every 8 turns
The outer ring of the board is consumed by the Storm every 8 turns. Pieces on Storm tiles are eliminated. The board shrinks from 8×8 toward 6×6 by turn 40. Pieces pushed to the edge by the Storm's advance are removed from play. Standard checkmate applies on the reduced board. Get to the circle. Stay in the circle.
"The board is getting smaller. Viktor is getting calmer. These things are unrelated."
Point Cost3 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: The Floor Is Lava — shrinking board forces pieces toward Lava
  • Conflict: The Small Council — 12 pawns + shrinking board = very crowded endgame

The four central squares (d4, d5, e4, e5) are the Swamp. Any piece entering the Swamp moves at 1 square per turn regardless of normal movement. Knights entering the Swamp are trapped for 2 turns. Capturing from the Swamp is permitted at reduced range. The Swamp is contested. The Swamp is disgusting. The Swamp is strategically essential.
"Viktor entered the Swamp to seize the centre. The Swamp seized Viktor. They are negotiating terms."

MOD-A007 ≈ Interstellar gravity / Gravity Falls
Gravitational Anomaly (Don't Think About It)
Tier S
▪ Board Mutation · Directional · Triggered
TRIGGER: Every 12 turns
Every 12 turns, gravity flips. All pieces slide 1 square toward the new "bottom" edge if that square is empty. Pieces sliding off the board are captured. Pawns re-orient to the new gravity direction. Kings slide too. The board tips with a full animation. Nobody ordered this. It happened anyway. Do not think about it too hard.
"Viktor's rook slid sideways. Viktor's pawns are now facing east. Viktor is not thinking about it."
Point Cost5 pts
Curse
ModesALL exc. Ranked S1
Interactions
  • Synergy: The Storm Closes In — gravity can push pieces off a shrunken board
  • Conflict: Winter Is Coming — frozen pieces do not slide
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Requires softlock testing with all piece types
MOD-A008 ≈ Black Mirror / Bandersnatch mirror world
Bandersnatch Protocol
Tier A
▪ Board Mutation · Structural · Persistent
TRIGGER: Match start (permanent)
The board is split by an invisible axis. Any move made on the left half is simultaneously mirrored on the right half for that piece. Pieces colliding on the axis swap positions. Kings are exempt. Both players are affected equally. Every choice echoes. Every move has a reflection. You chose this. You can't unchoose it.
"Viktor moved his bishop left. His bishop also moved right. Viktor is questioning the nature of his own free will."
Point Cost3 pts
Curse
ModesCasual · Story
Interactions
  • Synergy: Ghost Pieces — mirrored decoys compound information confusion
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Needs extensive edge case testing with castling logic

No, I Don't Think I Will

MOD-C008 ≈ Revenge plots / "I've been waiting" thriller trope
I've Been Waiting For This (En Passant Banked)
Tier B
▪ Active Ability · Pawn · Cooldown: 3 turns
ACTIVATE: Available when en passant is legal
En passant is no longer a one-turn window. Bank it. Store the capture option for up to 3 turns. Execute it whenever the moment is right — even if the opponent's pawn has since moved again. One banked en passant per pawn. The pawn glows faintly while holding it. The pawn has not forgotten. The pawn has been waiting for exactly the right moment. The pawn is ready.
"The pawn remembered. The pawn waited three turns. The pawn struck. 'I've been waiting for this,' the pawn communicated via movement."
Point Cost2 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Conflict: 50 First Moves — banked en passant lost during history blackout
  • Synergy: Retaliatory Swipe — backward capture + banked forward passant = extremely mobile pawns

Non-standard piece configurations. Different counts, different types, or entirely new piece behaviours. Both players see each other's army before the match begins.
MOD-E001 → MOD-E008
MOD-E001 ≈ Pigeon chess origin meme / Hatoful Boyfriend game
Hatoful Gambit (The Flock Army)
Tier S
▪ Asymmetric · New Piece Type · Knight Replacement
TRIGGER: Match start (one player)
Replace both Knights with Pigeon pieces. Pigeons move like Knights (L-shape) but every 5 turns each Pigeon announces (1 turn in advance) where it will autonomously hop — and then hops there regardless of your wishes. Hops can capture. The destination is visible to your opponent. Gerald will not sit on Pigeon pieces. Professional courtesy between birds.
"The pigeons are on the board. The pigeons have opinions about their positioning. Viktor is negotiating. The pigeons are not."
Point Cost5 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Did I Do That? — Pigeon pieces can also use the illegal rook-move ability
  • Synergy: It's Dark And I'm Scared — Pigeon hop destinations always visible through fog
MOD-E002 ≈ Les Misérables / Occupy Wall Street / pawn as working class
The Small Council (Do You Hear The Pawns Sing)
Tier A
▪ Asymmetric · Pawn Army · High Count
TRIGGER: Match start (one player)
Replace Knights and Bishops with 4 extra Pawns — total: 12 Pawns, King, Queen, 2 Rooks. No Knights, no Bishops. The 12-pawn side starts one rank forward (Rank 3). Promote normally. The opponent keeps their standard army. Do you hear the pawns sing? Yes. Yes you do. They are singing right now. They are marching. They have a flag.
"The pawns are singing. Viktor is uncomfortable with the energy this brings to an otherwise professional chess match."
Point Cost3 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Retaliatory Swipe — 12 pawns with backward capture creates a mobile wall
  • Conflict: The Storm Closes In — 12 pawns + shrinking board = gridlock (⚠ softlock risk)
MOD-E003 ≈ John Wick "there are three of them" / bodyguard trope
Yeah, There Are Three Of Them (Royal Guard)
Tier A
▪ Asymmetric · King-Centric · Defensive
TRIGGER: Match start (one player)
No Queen. Instead: 2 additional Kings (Royal Guards). Guards move like Kings and must also be kept out of check — but if a Guard is checkmated, it is removed from play rather than ending the game. The real King must still be protected normally. The opponent sees three Kings and has concerns. Their concerns are valid. There are three of them.
"Three kings on the board. The opponent looked at Viktor. Viktor looked at the board. 'Yeah,' Viktor said, 'there are three of them.'"
Point Cost3 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: Heavy Is The Crown — all three kings move slowly (cursed combo)
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Check/checkmate logic must tag the "real King" explicitly
MOD-E004 ≈ Fullmetal Alchemist "equivalent exchange" / overpowered anime character trope
Equivalent Exchange (The Chancellor)
Tier S
▪ Asymmetric · New Piece Type · Compound Mover
TRIGGER: Match start (replaces one Rook)
Replace one Rook with The Chancellor — a piece combining Rook and Knight movement. Each turn choose: move like a Rook (any distance orthogonally) OR like a Knight (L-shape leap). Not both. To gain this power, you sacrifice a Rook. Equivalent exchange. The Chancellor does not question the arrangement. The Chancellor simply arrives wherever it needs to be.
"The Chancellor can go anywhere. The Chancellor chose here. Viktor is thinking about why it chose here. Viktor is now scared."
Point Cost5 pts
Curse
ModesAll exc. Ranked S1
Interactions
  • Conflict: This Is My Swamp — Chancellor in Swamp limited to 1 square regardless of move type
  • Synergy: Did I Do That? — Chancellor using the Knight-ability as Rook move effectively covers the entire board in 2 turns
MOD-E005 ≈ Twin telepathy TikTok trend / "We share one brain" meme
Twin Telepathy (We Share One Brain)
Tier B
▪ Asymmetric · Linked Pieces · Symmetric Effect
TRIGGER: Match start (both players)
Both players' Bishops are telepathically linked. When one Bishop moves, the other automatically mirrors the direction (not destination) as far as legally possible. If the mirror is illegal, the secondary Bishop doesn't move. Applies to both players equally. They share one brain. The brain is doing its best. The brain is making decisions for both of them and not asking permission.
"Viktor moved his bishop diagonally. His other bishop also moved diagonally. They did this without communicating. Viktor finds this unsettling. The bishops find it natural."
Point Cost2 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Conflict: Forgive Me Bishop — confessing a Twin reveals its probable mirror move too
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Mirror move must resolve server-side; client must not predict before confirmation
MOD-E006 ≈ Kratos / God of War "BOY!" / rage-mode gaming
BOY. (The Berserker)
Tier A
▪ Asymmetric · Capture Chain · Aggressive
TRIGGER: On Berserker capture
One designated non-King piece is The Berserker. Whenever it captures a piece, it may immediately chain-capture if another legal capture is available — and keep going as long as captures exist. The chain continues indefinitely. If The Berserker is captured, its title passes to a random surviving piece. The Berserker does not choose to do this. The Berserker simply does this. BOY.
"The rook captured once. Then again. Then a third time. 'BOY.' said the rook, to nobody in particular. The board was mostly empty now."
Point Cost4 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Conflict: The Floor Is Lava — chain cannot continue if chain-land is on Lava
  • Synergy: FOR THE EMPEROR — Berserker Rook with cannon ability is extremely high value
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Chain capture termination condition must be explicit
MOD-E007 ≈ "Reject Modernity, Embrace Tradition" meme / underdog sports trope
Reject Queen, Embrace Knight (The Underpromoter)
Tier B
▪ Asymmetric · Promotion Rule · Strategic
TRIGGER: On pawn promotion (one player)
Your pawns cannot promote to Queen. Knight, Bishop, or Rook only. However, any promoted piece gains BOY. chain-capture for their first capture after promotion. Underpromotion is no longer the consolation prize. Underpromotion is the strategy. Reject the queen. Embrace the knight. The knight is ready. The knight has always been ready.
"It became a knight. Everyone in the room was surprised. The knight had been expecting this. The knight had been training."
Point Cost2 pts
Curse
ModesALL
Interactions
  • Synergy: BOY. (Berserker) — if Berserker active, promoted piece chains from first capture
  • Synergy: The Small Council — 12 unpromotable-to-queen pawns creates a fascinating strategic puzzle
MOD-E008 ≈ Moneyball / free agency / "I'll pay to get them back" sports market
Moneyball (We'll Buy Them Back)
Tier A
▪ Asymmetric · Captured Piece Reuse · Economy
TRIGGER: On opponent capturing your piece
Each piece you lose generates a Mercenary Token. Spend a Token (costs your turn) to drop a captured piece back onto any empty square as your own. Cannot drop to give check. Cannot drop pawns on Rank 1 or 8. Max 3 Tokens held. The piece asks no questions about working conditions. The piece is simply back. This is Moneyball. You've done the analysis. The market inefficiency is your captured rook.
"Viktor lost a bishop. Viktor ran the numbers. Viktor bought the bishop back at a discount. The bishop did not know it was traded. The bishop will not know."
Point Cost4 pts
Curse
ModesAll exc. Ranked S1
Interactions
  • Conflict: They Were The Traitor — defected piece captured = can be bought back (YES, this is the ruling)
  • Synergy: This Is Necessary (The Snap) — sacrificed pieces generate Tokens (economy loop)
  • ⚠ Balance Flag: Drop-check guard is critical — server-side validation required every placement

Modifier Design Rules
Unchanged from v0.1. Every modifier satisfies these before entering production.
RULE 01
The Laugh Test
Every modifier must produce at least one moment per match that makes a player laugh, groan, or say "what?" out loud. Frustration without delight = rewrite.
RULE 02
The Rematch Test
After losing with a modifier active, the loser should want to play again — not quit. Consistently produces "I want to uninstall" = nerf or remove.
RULE 03
The Legibility Rule
Every modifier effect must be understandable at a glance. If an effect requires reading a tooltip mid-game, simplify it.
RULE 04
The King Is Sacred
No modifier may directly capture, permanently disable, or hide the King without explicit checkmate resolution logic. Every King-touching modifier requires double review.
RULE 05
Server Authority
All modifier logic executes server-side. No exceptions. Client renders only. Anti-cheat starts here.
RULE 06
No Softlocks
Every interaction that could theoretically prevent game-end must be tested. Softlock potential = combination banned or forced-draw rule added.
RULE 07
Balance Patch Cadence
Point costs adjust in 0.5pt increments every 2 weeks. No modifier above 60% or below 40% win rate in its bracket after 2 weeks of live data.
RULE 08
Curse Must Pay
Every Cursed modifier must grant enough negative points that a skilled player can build a viable loadout around the extra budget.
RULE 09
Ranked Pool Curation
Not every modifier enters Ranked every season. New modifiers spend one season in Casual-only before Ranked eligibility.
RULE 10
Interactions Are Features
Surprising modifier interactions are content, not bugs. Document all discovered combos. Patch only interactions that violate Rules 04 or 06.
